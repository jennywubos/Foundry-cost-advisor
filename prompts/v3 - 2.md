## Commitment Readiness Logic Table

| Condition | Demand Signal | Buffer after commitment | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| available < requested | any | negative | ❌ NO-GO — insufficient capacity |
| available >= requested | LOW | > 50% | ✅ GO |
| available >= requested | LOW / MEDIUM | 30–50% | ✅ GO |
| available >= requested | MEDIUM | 10–30% | ⚠️ CAUTION — sufficient but tight |
| available >= requested | HIGH | any | ⚠️ CAUTION — high demand, lock capacity now |
| available >= requested | CRITICAL | any | ⚠️ CAUTION — extremely tight, act immediately |
| available >= requested | any | < 10% | ❌ NO-GO — buffer too small, risk of capacity loss |

### Definitions
- **Buffer after commitment** = (availablePTUs − requestedPTUs) / totalPTUs
- **Demand Signal** = current availablePTUs / totalPTUs BEFORE new commitment:
  - CRITICAL : ≤ 5% available
  - HIGH     : 5–20% available
  - MEDIUM   : 20–50% available
  - LOW      : > 50% available
